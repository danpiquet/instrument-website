Battle of the Instruments

Our first instrument is the cello. Behold its glory. The only reason the cello has taken second chair to the violin is because it is a far more capable instrument in terms of range of tonalities.

Up next is the violin. A beatuiful instrument, no doubt, unfortunately incapable of lower tonalities that are more pleasing to the ear.

The foundation of the orchestra is the bass. It may be the most boring instrument to play, but it is certainly one of the most important.

Last and certainly least is the viola. Notice the player is all alone. That is because they contribute nothing and they might as well be playing alone.
